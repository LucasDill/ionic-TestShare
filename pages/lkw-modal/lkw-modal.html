Tips

How to gather details
of the patient's
Last Known Well


Remember:
LKW may not be when
symptoms where first observed

LKW is when the patient was last
at their baseline function
NOT when stroke symptoms started
(note: it can the same if it
was witnessed in person).


This app supports a navigation system based on "time windows"
to potential acute treatment.
A valid time is required
to proceed through the app.



Creatively inquire
 of patient or family:


When did you see them last?


When did you talk to them last?


What where they doing last?


What do they normally do
at that time of day? Earlier?


Did you notice anything that would indicate they had been in a normal routine?
